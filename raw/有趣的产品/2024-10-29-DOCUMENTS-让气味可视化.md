# DOCUMENTS / 让气味可视化

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_gif%2FoSxy99diagQiaz3zkAicRGrW12wibyicw3LrOVH1LFYkgKNXHHlKdRzLpNu7EqUpmfKr2r23x6xvf0eZmSYldnP7VbQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dgif%26from%3Dappmsg)  

DOCUMENTS意为「记录与文献」，取其谐音，寓意「值得被记住的气味与馈赠」，「闻献DOCUMENTS」由此而生。


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFR1dz8hlKOTzJ3miaGY0YwTa2j8qWJgy7ia9mJeUtaQYJsjjcssvu9oEg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFLVUTST1xS0eImG9CZFlPJVjNLCusN1AfywsJAcFIABibAaliaNWZPNnw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

品牌有意择取与记录、文献相关的文化元素，在深挖其历史后，将它们在视觉、设计、概念等方向进行运用，从而形成与「闻献DOCUMENTS」强关联的品牌基因。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFKWw3NgWyniagDLemKF8Z8gMHQQZsvTNQBTP2LQcaBhy6W2Ly1o4Jn1w%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_gif%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFfAfMcNxTBicHQbd1HUXadGLbmNQ7xqib2jdKH1If56quUE5FoShP9veg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dgif%26from%3Dappmsg)

「闻献DOCUMENTS」作为国内沙龙香氛品牌，其与市场上商业化成功的香氛品牌相比，最大的区别在于：初创团队由一支「纯设计」团队构成，没有专业的销售团队。


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFtspe0Lujjpb7eh2z9BVFDxAetXz0O78hBSPibd1D2z1Yeug5RaEotkA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_png%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFicbgAXJsZ5kZwYSiaVEBnbe8vdms7Svpp7Rl0P2AKv8roI4uWcsVPHCw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dpng%26from%3Dappmsg)

主理人孟昭然，涉猎过多个艺术领域，包括绘画、摄影和花艺设计等，他在相关采访中他提到：闻献的用户画像比较宽泛，针对了设计师、艺术家、中产以及高净值消费人群。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFVpxFDMsiaUzgvYVaq9xVD3M3e7UUiaM0dvK9cNE4bpxQiaqVCiakUWtnIQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFlVNibbr5hN6LxfECcibH3SpaI2ml40AbibnvXbeTIVFfIE3xAwJAtB0Nw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

对于一个既想要展现独特自我，又有消费力和鉴赏力的年轻群体来说，如今的香水市场仿佛还有一些缺失，孟昭然认为他们似乎还可以有更好的选择。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_gif%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFaCngia3TbKAjRaAicAs3DJXkzia388gCNAglOyDqk5BqLT1LhAXDMknqw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dgif%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFtOhcp9bIkf2ZORLkT1KrJ2kzHKQgjs3S7w7M8kpgicicwpH1TwwjWUyg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

创立「闻献DOCUMENTS」的契机，源于孟昭然在深入探索国外高端香氛品牌市场时的一个观察。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFLYB1RZU0icQ7y2SdPuGNrXicdlu5HqTTJWHjBUytOk5WyR0iaOibbNeR2Q%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_png%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrF5zEB0GZ94f39PFMUiaKYaWS2s3PibQib4rt3jS7OhDoWgmlMe4ewttVrg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dpng%26from%3Dappmsg)

他逐渐发现，尽管这些品牌以高昂的价格标榜其独特的品味和社交属性，但往往难以真正满足消费者多样化的个人需求。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrF1za1RibhCCPXQiabbAJxaUMnBibI1wxJrgzRiaEDeXnFicKpH8PEOXFhHwA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFYeQl3T1IoZNXOBVS0fMibuolA3qB43O86nk3ocKyvJ6TZ2mIxHkcicww%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

高端香氛品牌往往侧重于塑造一种统一、高端的品牌形象，其产品设计和推广策略往往围绕这一核心展开。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFX28eAWppBHoNR5PK0T4DhjZLPdTbTlrOM9nLHreHnVqWiaFeNZ7hO4Q%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrF8W8vCyOXKQ38IibAo5cGp5ZyCcbvohfBUGUVofL7x6e32G396M2eARA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

每个人的个性、情感和生活经历都是独一无二的，这种统一化的品牌形象和产品设计难以与每个消费者的内心世界产生深层次的共鸣。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_gif%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFicsds4icry5ibCsgLOgTbVqu0Q1XwZdIxibCVp4pxLGHqlcPWeEygjZ3eA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dgif%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFVaJ0mBJKmcoaZKrrrNib388jGT6NicK0RYXmRNGPNO9BvujtYxWabe2Q%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

人们的审美观念和消费习惯也在不断变化，个人需求的泛化趋势日益明显。消费者不再仅仅满足于产品本身的功能和品质，更加注重产品所能带来的情感价值和文化认同感。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrF5icNDbZIgkE8vH0KNhDUdiaiaVhMPf9fZY89mNkvdv5xic98EOwMuibytbQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFP1wCXgSfc4Pv5qoZsnEavBgnic8MvXicaibp4NB1NLIsSMslsYHfPDLWA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

香水作为一种高度私人化的产品，它的气味和感官体验，本应成为满足这种个性化需求的重要载体。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_gif%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFzBd3pQiaLbicLUUfFMeTgxZh3oO8VzpPmqWFYj7tyu4sDXoficyxu7TOw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dgif%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFH6bWyW4dqMNGbZCicmpM6IG6QRkib90iaGgqriatOVY6GKE9pgV34ibqoKA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

许多国外高端香氛品牌却未能充分发掘这一潜力，导致消费者难以找到真正符合自己个性和情感需求的产品。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFybCB0C7K6yMRzxlwCeaXdcfzNJTianQCGXnG5eIKcOWQ8It9q0ic19sg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFicBoQdWyCJN1r578fpp5IrEwicDdWRVMqv9VJ3zMUibkjBOEO6bTib0TaQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

孟昭然希望通过这个品牌，打破传统香氛市场的束缚，以气味为媒介，放大个性，记录并传递每个人的独特情感和故事。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrFTibkogQdwia1Bl3X7jglicz2OvNHyqJkxI76mVoibbKqWSsX9xWOmYqOmw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrF3ADtFCCle4W0tic491fCZA2nZZhG5VIUP1FhT9dZlKKib8n4BtUdgBoA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

同时他也希望通过「闻献DOCUMENTS」能够带动本土文化和民族的共鸣感，让更多人通过香水这一「华而不实」的载体，感受到自己与本土文化的深厚联系和独特魅力。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fsz_mmbiz_jpg%2FoSxy99diagQiaib3V1ibvusKfmvibfBwpibtrF0Ye8iccRpyPeOiczR4AXLDkbkzlvfRyp60hMV2JBgcDonBmiciaL5EBmhw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)
