# 不是？聂永真就设计了2条线，甲方竟然超超超满意...

[mp.weixin.qq.com](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=MzA4MzI2ODAyMw==&mid=2649511245&idx=1&sn=a02976ae57a84eb4bcbe629f31273818&chksm=863eea371508abedd8c93fe76b6e664a3ec23a1db1e6cbebc3d145a60e2a7768c20a38ce0620&mpshare=1&scene=1&srcid=0924bWjylq3SJjDU5cybIZRr&sharer_shareinfo=f04c525c9dbb0de3839b83aefe67dba0&sharer_shareinfo_first=f04c525c9dbb0de3839b83aefe67dba0#rd)玩点儿酷的视觉 设计INNER


预计2025年完工的中国台中市立美术馆，由日本建筑师**妹岛和世** 与**西泽立卫**打造，拥有8个独特功能和美学空间。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFPN8nqvAyaGqB7LPMSXFsERNkspZUyjejeXbkbwu9znCD79yOhZfScQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

而这座别具一格的文化场域的识别系统则由知名设计师**聂永真**的永真急制Workshop操刀。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFStFsdoQNzuyIh1PdXsk1ErtTaZpuX3ve22ZXSicN3P3IsiblibDcOgmvg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)


识别设计以整体建筑「绿美图」作为主品牌，串连起座落其中的两座场馆------「台中市立美术馆」和「台中市立图书馆」。


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFKMoZPpBsOwg33hhhNVsUl66bcCQ0ZUDxdSKp1hhogzg0K5utzRlcbg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFQeTTQTnlcxibfZgibbGeOYr40fQaMdJ0xAmpEIwzOgmzMb48ERcAvarg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFBJiaK5DY2Vqejj8wiaKbfibcAF4jGPS5GE6kMaL311kaqibhXDKuHicdosQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

设计上延续了他一如既往的极简设计哲学，只是这次，**他又做减法了，设计上虽只剩下几条线，但甲方却满意的不行...**

这是为啥？

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFoQGO0vMxd5ANibqeoZjjSeQ9d6dIqUUvXXYricFJWepbYc6VrYrYGDMg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)


首先，此次的识别系统是聂永真以建筑本体作为灵感进行的延伸设计。他提到，一个好的识别设计需要具备五个条件⬇️

***包括独特（Distinctive）、难忘（Memorable）、适应性强（Adaptable）、简单（Simple）及永恒（Timeless），***台中绿美图品牌识别正是在这样的脉络下诞生。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFQzVpd1socOYYvmPeSsicsyiawQxQP1ztLZ2d9LODu12vkoJ2Nd4nkdog%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

看似简单的两条"双杠"，其结构以及恰到好处的留白，除了体现建筑方正的特性之外，也意味着建筑与建筑、空间与空间之间的无限可能。


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFRrF1LSpljnOib0bnhIkPcIAMsH1lGRviatPskau0dEJCUmHaPXlgGwtQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFd4kzyDK0rSjAQHJPGxOicHTeNqiak1S8cMsntteDkHyaHnjYGCRLR92g%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

虽然许多人看后可能会有争议------"这不就是两条线？"

但聂永真却表示*"这个识别看似简单，实则隐喻了场域之间彼此跨域融合的众多可能性"。*

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFQaWpVqWMnKlOG3lPz9CYS5hmN6yxt5AWiaUYboDPB7UxlKWVhYzicYTQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

除了品牌识别主体之外，聂永真也针对「台中市立图书馆」以及「台中市立美术馆」分别进行Logo 的设计。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFYDVcCp0ugpL7XtDBe7sujib3yboL6gkPJ50rhSCc6NKlaY0HonYeFkQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

前者以类书籍摆放于书架上（并且有些直立、有些倾倒）的具象化概念，象征「俩杠」之间承载着知识与书习；

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFSWd78TUfek84soI4M8HzicEW1kiaNVF1KICUMydFGlb5icfw0nzSMibjow%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

后者则以连续性的折线呈现出美术馆在未来策展上的无限可能。

聂永真认为，绿美图的独特性在于，两馆各自独立却又相互连结，形构出崭新的空间体验。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFic8ONtrwe0FjmEX7qXhKYq7oSIF7hicRxJfyclLpOO8IT45vQnGvOfwA%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dwebp%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFqn4JDQbBFWry3IQv13vB7XnJg98NwkOVEaibKxKuQ6a3JiaqicQ13icTyg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dwebp%26from%3Dappmsg)

台中市立美术馆长赖依欣认为，视觉识别系统是美术馆建立独特且清晰品牌定位的重要基础。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFfZr4j2KHsVFtRKQbdrpZ5BQ4Q0TrXY9AEAcdlelTJ67Ug9NQL2BF1w%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)


作为一座城市美术馆，台中市立美术馆未来将透过多元跨域的艺术策展、典藏与推广计划，与在地对话，并接轨国际。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFtFODz7BJZQDPbXcjB5FXZoAlO8y1KiaOs8XaQUJM2f6YxmTdpc4WpLg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)


美术馆未来也将积极回应绿美图的场域特色，与图书馆进行多面向合作，激发美术跟图书、知识的碰撞交流，带给观众不同层次的艺术参与体验。

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFSl03HXE0ian62Y3bsf1z0s35I9zmHQap4Rliaklb4Y3KJy2qAQ9AJJ1g%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dwebp%26from%3Dappmsg)


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFvdNjwg7l6AxgAq1yYObcGEvlLZIGemu8GsBtCJtyyOdBdtUWNZ5t3Q%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

试问这样的甲方，哪里找？ 开个玩笑。  


聂永真的作品一向带有极强的辨识度和个人风格，点、线、面在他手中更是变成了设计的音符⬇️  


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFCaFMdLqjialGO7SdNfNpzjyffo57dricakneLyDYCpbE1HWG5Fe2G0tw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFUiac1iaHssovXnASQm4MWY1A4MNfbYQUSfp7J13Znbaa4QvkGKzsMZWw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)


作为影响一代人的设计师，他也曾为周杰伦、李宗盛、林宥嘉等等大咖设计专辑封面⬇️  


![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFxic7LT27DSGBfEHaicp7mn2v5Pfhtj9K5gkPeu5AoNX9VK6wzR0tSnJw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFFxbPYcBoUjuYTTxR2U5fM1G4Xk1MclpQNzfPRiauxX3tJZm5OY5glnQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFDXHcw60wtAdYI5gicvlK6Xj1FXDe1IgtJjCVAf43ibuicOXJfiaqOaHglQ%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_jpg%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlF4fIRZdNEjXa7Ix0hM8ibCXp3gC9fPfhIMAL9xZOaszfb3jLQsvx4Sbw%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Djpeg%26from%3Dappmsg)

设计与艺术的界限在当下已经越来越模糊。

像几年前**原研哉为小米** 200万设计的Logo也被网友调侃**"设计的真好，跟没设计一样"，**但却拥有了超高的话题热度⬇️

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmmbiz.qpic.cn%2Fmmbiz_png%2FGiaM51p20L20g97sY2e2MNiao1iaOCaFrlFezwq7uRfQG0YYibNusibZsqpXXL7kAkaYEz3cjQWQicgUGuKIL65hpKwg%2F640%3Fwx_fmt%3Dpng%26from%3Dappmsg)

台湾地区近些年一直提倡用设计让城市更美好，也对设计师给予了非常大的创作空间。这也是"双杠"可以通过的原因之一...

所以，大家觉得聂永真这两条线的设计又该如何评价呢？
