## Recommendations for a successful onboarding on AI-driven products
成功引入人工智能驱动产品的建议

## 1. Encourage sample tasks upfront
1. 鼓励预先完成示例任务

Use the homepage to minimize the time to value (TTV) by showcasing the value proposition of your product right away. Be strategic in encouraging users to engage with your product and take that initial step forward.  
使用主页立即展示产品的价值主张，从而最大限度地缩短价值实现时间 (TTV)。策略性地鼓励用户与您的产品互动并向前迈出第一步。

Research has shown that users who have taken the initiative to begin onboarding are generally more engaged and motivated to get value from the product. **They have overcome the initial inertia of starting something new.**   
研究表明，主动开始使用的用户通常更积极地从产品中获取价值。**他们克服了开始新事物的最初惯性。**

Consider how you can simplify your product to reduce cognitive load. Avoid overwhelming users with too many features at once. Instead, focus on clear homepage to enhance the user experience.  
考虑如何简化产品以减少认知负担。避免同时使用太多功能让用户感到不知所措。相反，应专注于清晰的主页以增强用户体验。

🙋‍♂️ **Tip:** Allow users to interact with the AI product during the onboarding. Provide sample tasks, prompts, or scenarios that demonstrate the product's functionality, letting users see it in action.  
🙋‍♂️**提示：**允许用户在入门过程中与 AI 产品进行交互。提供演示产品功能的示例任务、提示或场景，让用户看到它的实际效果。

**✨ Example** : [Perplexity AI](http://perplexity.ai/) --- Their incredibly simple homepage highlights "How is Perplexity AI different?" This approach helps users get started, see positive results, and understand their value proposition and competitive positioning. It's a win-win, right?  
**✨ 示例** ： [Perplexity AI](http://perplexity.ai/) --- 他们极其简单的主页突出显示"Perplexity AI 有何不同？"这种方法可以帮助用户入门、看到积极的结果并了解他们的价值主张和竞争定位。这是双赢，对吧？

## 2. Provide general task examples instead of specific ones
2. 提供一般任务示例而不是具体任务示例

While it might seem counterintuitive, it's often better to offer broad examples that showcase the tool's capabilities without being too niche.  
虽然这看起来可能违反直觉，但通常最好提供广泛的示例来展示该工具的功能，但又不会太小众。

For example, if your tool is prompt-based, use straightforward prompts like "Help me generate text" or "Help me generate images" rather than overly specific tasks such as "*Draft a viral love song that echoes Romeo and Juliet's tale, but imagine Shakespeare was a comedian"* (as seen in [SongMaker on ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-txEiClD5G-song-maker)).  
例如，如果您的工具是基于提示的，请使用简单的提示，例如"帮我生成文本"或"帮我生成图像"，而不是过于具体的任务，例如"*起草一首与罗密欧和朱丽叶的故事相呼应的病毒情歌，但想象一下莎士比亚是一位喜剧演员"* （如[ChatGPT 上的 SongMaker](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-txEiClD5G-song-maker)所示）。

**🙋‍♂️ Tip:** Users are usually eager to explore and might skip long instructions. Instead, provide concise, relevant information that quickly answers essential questions like "What does this do?" and "How does it work?"  
**🙋‍♂️ 提示：**用户通常渴望探索，可能会跳过冗长的说明。相反，提供简洁、相关的信息，快速回答诸如"这是做什么的？"之类的基本问题。以及"它是如何运作的？"

**✨ Example** : If your tool targets a distinct persona, feel free to use specific examples to demonstrate results. For instance, Photoshop AI offers both general explanations and specific examples on how to achieve better results for image generation.  
**✨ 示例**：如果您的工具针对不同的角色，请随意使用具体示例来演示结果。例如，Photoshop AI 提供了有关如何获得更好的图像生成结果的一般说明和具体示例。

## 3. Address privacy and security concerns
3.解决隐私和安全问题

AI products often require access to sensitive data, so it's better to **communicate data usage in the first interactions.** Address privacy and security concerns upfront. *Explain how the data is used, stored, and protected to build trust and alleviate potential concerns.*   
人工智能产品通常需要访问敏感数据，因此最好**在第一次交互时传达数据使用情况。** 提前解决隐私和安全问题。*解释如何使用、存储和保护数据以建立信任并减轻潜在的担忧。*

🙋‍♂️ **Tip:** Make privacy settings easy to find and adjust. Offer users straightforward options to manage their data preferences and clearly outline any third-party data sharing, so they feel in control and informed.  
🙋‍♂️**提示：**让隐私设置易于查找和调整。为用户提供简单的选项来管理他们的数据偏好，并清楚地概述任何第三方数据共享，让他们感到掌控和知情。

**✨ Example:** Often, personal finance apps include a dedicated section during onboarding that explains data encryption, privacy measures, and how users can control their data-sharing preferences.  
**✨ 示例：**通常，个人理财应用程序在用户使用过程中会包含一个专门的部分，解释数据加密、隐私措施以及用户如何控制其数据共享偏好。
