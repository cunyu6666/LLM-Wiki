## The evolution of user interfaces
用户界面的演变

Before we discuss how AI-powered human-computer interactions look today, let's take a step back to understand the evolution of [user interfaces and the approach to their design](https://www.pragmaticcoders.com/blog/introduction-to-user-interface-design).  
在我们讨论今天人工智能驱动的人机交互是什么样子之前，让我们退后一步来了解[用户界面的演变及其设计方法](https://www.pragmaticcoders.com/blog/introduction-to-user-interface-design)。

## Punch card interfaces
打孔卡接口

Imagine a world where interacting with a computer required meticulously punching holes into stiff cards, each one representing a step in a complex process.  
想象一下这样一个世界：与计算机交互需要在硬卡片上小心翼翼地打孔，每个孔都代表复杂过程中的一个步骤。

This was the reality in 1945, a time when computing was confined to the realm of experts and data centers. Users would prepare a batch of punched cards, submit them, and wait, often until the next day, to see if their instructions had been executed correctly. A single error could render the entire process futile, producing no output or meaningless results.  
这就是 1945 年的现实，当时计算仅限于专家和数据中心的领域。用户会准备一批打孔卡，提交它们，然后等待（通常要等到第二天），看看他们的指令是否被正确执行。一个错误可能会使整个过程徒劳无功，产生任何输出或毫无意义的结果。

## Command-line interfaces 命令行界面

Fast forward to 1964, and a revolution had begun.  
快进到 1964 年，一场革命已经开始。

The advent of the command-line interface transformed the way people interacted with computers. Instead of submitting a rigid set of instructions all at once, users could now input commands one by one and receive immediate feedback. This iterative process allowed for reassessment and adjustment, paving the way for more dynamic and interactive computing experiences.  
命令行界面的出现改变了人们与计算机交互的方式。用户现在可以逐条输入命令并立即收到反馈，而不是一次提交一组严格的指令。这个迭代过程允许重新评估和调整，为更加动态和交互式的计算体验铺平道路。

## Graphic user interfaces 图形用户界面

The next leap forward came with the **introduction of graphical user interfaces (GUIs)** .  
下一次飞跃是**图形用户界面(GUI) 的引入**。

These interfaces democratized computing, making it accessible to the masses. Users no longer needed to memorize and type complex commands; they could simply click and drag their way through tasks. This shift not only enhanced usability but also spurred the widespread adoption of personal computers.  
这些接口使计算民主化，使其可供大众使用。用户不再需要记住和输入复杂的命令；他们只需单击并拖动即可完成任务。这种转变不仅增强了可用性，而且刺激了个人计算机的广泛采用。

## Today: AI interfaces 今天：人工智能接口

![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmiro.medium.com%2Fv2%2Fresize%3Afit%3A1400%2F0%2AEXupuknhjIX3ZfaL.png&valid=false)

Today, we stand on the brink of another transformative era in human-computer interaction.  
今天，我们正站在人机交互的另一个变革时代的边缘。

Jakob Nielsen, a leading expert in web usability, posits that **AI represents the next major user-interface paradigm** .  
网络可用性领域的领先专家雅各布·尼尔森 (Jakob Nielsen)认为**人工智能代表了下一个主要的用户界面范例**。

Unlike previous interfaces that required users to specify detailed commands, AI focuses on understanding and fulfilling desired outcomes. Instead of instructing a computer to open a browser, search for a blog, and select an article, **users will soon be able to simply express their intent, and the AI will handle the rest** .  
与以前要求用户指定详细命令的界面不同，人工智能专注于理解和实现期望的结果。**用户很快将能够简单地表达他们的意图，而无需指示计算机打开浏览器、搜索博客和选择文章，而人工智能将处理剩下的事情**。

## Problem: Usability issues of current implementation
问题：当前实施的可用性问题

However, the current state of AI interfaces is not without its challenges.  
然而，人工智能接口的现状并非没有挑战。

Most AI tools rely on prompt-based inputs, which can be a significant barrier for many users. **Crafting effective prompts demands a high degree of articulation** , a skill that a large portion of the global population lacks.  
大多数人工智能工具依赖于基于提示的输入，这对许多用户来说可能是一个重大障碍。**制作有效的提示需要高度的表达能力**，而全球很大一部分人缺乏这种技能。

In fact, **nearly half of the world's adults are considered low- literacy users** , with reading skills below a sixth-grade level.   
事实上，**世界上近一半的成年人被认为是低识字用户**，阅读能力低于六年级水平。This issue is not confined to developing countries; in the United States alone, 54% of adults have a literacy below a sixth-grade level, from which 21% are functionally illiterate.  
这个问题不仅限于发展中国家；仅在美国，54% 的成年人识字率低于六年级水平，其中 21% 为功能性文盲。

Even for those who are literate, creating precise prompts can be cognitively demanding and time-consuming. This situation mirrors the early days of command-line interfaces, where only those with specialized knowledge could effectively communicate with computers.  
即使对于那些有文化的人来说，创建精确的提示也可能对认知要求很高且耗时。这种情况反映了早期的命令行界面，只有具有专业知识的人才能有效地与计算机进行通信。

Just as those interfaces required trained query specialists, today's AI systems often necessitate the expertise of prompt engineers to yield the best results.  
正如这些界面需要训练有素的查询专家一样，当今的人工智能系统通常需要及时工程师的专业知识才能产生最佳结果。

## Solution: Hybrid interfaces
解决方案：混合接口

Looking ahead, the future of AI promises a more intuitive and accessible [user experience](https://www.pragmaticcoders.com/blog/ux-explained-what-is-it-how-much-does-it-cost-do-i-need-it).  
展望未来，人工智能的未来有望提供更直观、更易于访问的[用户体验](https://www.pragmaticcoders.com/blog/ux-explained-what-is-it-how-much-does-it-cost-do-i-need-it)。

We are **moving towards hybrid interfaces that combine the powerful capabilities of AI with the user-friendly nature of graphical interfaces** . Major tech companies like OpenAI, Google, and Apple are already showcasing how these integrations can look, blending AI's intent-based functionalities with familiar, easy-to- use graphical elements.  
我们正在**走向混合界面，将人工智能的强大功能与图形界面的用户友好性结合起来** 。 OpenAI 、谷歌和苹果等主要科技公司已经展示了这些集成的外观，将人工智能基于意图的功能与熟悉、易于使用的图形元素融合在一起。

These advancements will not only make AI more accessible but also seamlessly integrate it into our daily workflows.  
这些进步不仅使人工智能更容易使用，而且还将其无缝集成到我们的日常工作流程中。

Imagine a world where your computer understands your needs with minimal effort on your part, allowing you to focus on your goals rather than the mechanics of achieving them.   
想象一下这样一个世界：您的计算机只需付出最少的努力就能了解您的需求，从而使您能够专注于自己的目标，而不是实现目标的机制。The future of human-computer interaction is bright, and it's poised to make technology more intuitive and inclusive than ever before.  
人机交互的未来是光明的，它将使技术比以往任何时候都更加直观和包容。

## AI interface design: Opportunities and challenges
AI界面设计：机遇与挑战

The advent of AI opens a world of exciting opportunities for human-computer interaction, but it also presents new challenges for UX designers.  
人工智能的出现为人机交互开辟了一个充满令人兴奋的机会的世界，但它也给用户体验设计师带来了新的挑战。

To harness AI effectively, designers must become well-versed in core machine learning concepts, understanding both the capabilities and limitations of these systems. This knowledge will enable them to identify user needs that AI can address and to continuously revisit and reassess these needs as AI technology rapidly evolves.  
为了有效地利用人工智能，设计人员必须精通核心机器学习概念，了解这些系统的功能和局限性。这些知识将使他们能够识别人工智能可以解决的用户需求，并随着人工智能技术的快速发展不断重新审视和重新评估这些需求。

**Designing for AI involves accommodating a probabilistic system that can produce unexpected or unexplainable results.** This means designers can't plan for every possible scenario in the user journey. Instead, they must collaborate closely with engineers to shape the potential outputs, ensuring that the results align with user expectations and requirements.  
**人工智能设计涉及适应可能产生意外或无法解释结果的概率系统。**这意味着设计人员无法为用户旅程中的每种可能场景进行规划。相反，他们必须与工程师密切合作来塑造潜在的输出，确保结果符合用户的期望和要求。

The traditional definition of a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) often shifts in the context of AI-driven products.  
最小可行产品 (MVP)的传统定义经常在人工智能驱动产品的背景下发生变化。 **A beautiful and user-friendly interface is meaningless if the AI system's output doesn't deliver value to the user** .  
**如果人工智能系统的输出不能为用户带来价值，那么漂亮且用户友好的界面就没有意义。**

Therefore, **the primary focus should be on the quality and relevance of the AI's outpu** t. Once this foundation is solid, designers can then build a compelling and usable product around it.  
因此，**主要关注点应该是人工智能输出的质量和相关性**。一旦这个基础稳固，设计师就可以围绕它构建引人注目且可用的产品。
![](https://cubox.pro/c/filters:no_upscale()?imageUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fmiro.medium.com%2Fv2%2Fresize%3Afit%3A1400%2F0%2Ai6HK35jfVFRXDV-f.png&valid=false)

## Visual language of AI
AI视觉语言

As AI rapidly gains traction in the digital world, companies are eager to integrate AI into their products or develop AI-centric solutions. This rush has led designers to emphasize AI capabilities and create distinctive visual identities.  
随着人工智能在数字世界中迅速受到关注，企业渴望将人工智能集成到其产品中或开发以人工智能为中心的解决方案。这种热潮促使设计师强调人工智能功能并创造独特的视觉识别。

Interestingly, **there are noticeable trends in how AI is presented across various products** .  
有趣的是，**人工智能在各种产品中的呈现方式存在明显的趋势**。

## Color trends 色彩趋势

A striking trend in AI design is **the prevalence of purple and purple-ish gradients** .  
人工智能设计的一个显着趋势是**紫色和紫色渐变的盛行**。

Purple, adjacent to the commonly used blue, offers a sense of familiarity while remaining distinct and rarely used in other interfaces. The AI boom coincided with the rise of the Linear app, which prominently features purple, further cementing its association with AI.  
紫色与常用的蓝色相邻，给人一种熟悉的感觉，同时保持独特且很少在其他界面中使用。人工智能的繁荣恰逢 Linear 应用程序的兴起，该应用程序以紫色为特色，进一步巩固了其与人工智能的联系。

This phenomenon mirrors the earlier trends seen during the cryptocurrency and NFT surge, where purple and gradients were similarly used to signal something new and innovative. AI-focused companies like Jasper and Copy.ai have adopted purple as their primary branding color, while others like Notion and Figjam use purple to highlight AI features. GitHub Copilot and Dora AI employ gradients to convey modernity.  
这种现象反映了加密货币和NFT激增期间的早期趋势，其中紫色和渐变同样被用来表示新的和创新的东西。 Jasper 和 Copy.ai 等专注于人工智能的公司采用紫色作为主要品牌颜色，而Notion和Figjam等其他公司则使用紫色来突出人工智能功能。 GitHub Copilot 和Dora AI使用渐变来传达现代感。

While this trend is prevalent, some products are beginning to forge their own identities, moving away from the purple and gradient dominance. Companies like Musho, Reword, and Hume AI have developed unique branding that diverges from the current trends, suggesting a sense of permanence and individuality.  
虽然这种趋势很普遍，但一些产品开始打造自己的特色，摆脱紫色和渐变的主导地位。 Musho 、Reword 和Hume AI 等公司开发了与当前趋势不同的独特品牌，暗示着永恒和个性的感觉。

## Iconography 图像学

For many established products, integrating AI features is more subtle.  
对于许多成熟的产品来说，集成人工智能功能更加微妙。

Instead of overhauling their design, they incorporate AI through nuanced elements like iconography and loading animations. Figma and Apple exemplify this approach, seamlessly blending AI capabilities into their existing interfaces without overwhelming the user with bold colors or dramatic changes.  
他们没有彻底改变设计，而是通过图像和加载动画等细致入微的元素融入人工智能。 Figma和Apple就是这种方法的例证，它们将人工智能功能无缝地融入到他们现有的界面中，而不会因大胆的颜色或戏剧性的变化而让用户感到不知所措。

In terms of iconography, **sparkling stars have emerged as a popular indicator of AI features** . Companies like Gemini and Story.com have integrated these stars into their logos. Traditional icons are often embellished with sparkling stars to subtly signal AI functionalities.  
在图像方面，**闪闪发光的星星已成为人工智能功能的流行指标**。 Gemini 和 Story.com 等公司已将这些星星融入到他们的徽标中。传统图标通常饰有闪闪发光的星星，以巧妙地暗示人工智能功能。

Additionally,**orbs and robots** are frequently used to represent AI assistants and co- pilots. Notable examples include GitHub Copilot, Character.ai, and Spotify's DJ agent. These symbols effectively communicate the supportive and interactive nature of AI.  
此外，**球体和机器人** 经常被用来代表人工智能助手和副驾驶。著名的例子包括GitHub Copilot 、Character.ai 和Spotify的 DJ 代理。这些符号有效地传达了人工智能的支持性和交互性。

## Open ended prompt input 开放式提示输入

**Prompt input remains the most popular way to harness AI capabilities** , offering users the freedom to input any query or command. However, this freedom can also be overwhelming due to the sheer number of possibilities. To enhance the user experience and mitigate this challenge, several strategies can be implemented.  
**提示输入仍然是利用人工智能功能的最流行的方式** ，让用户可以自由地输入任何查询或命令。然而，由于可能性的数量巨大，这种自由也可能是压倒性的。为了增强用户体验并缓解这一挑战，可以实施多种策略。

1. Firstly, providing **examples** can help users understand how to effectively interact with the AI. By showing concrete instances of prompts and their outcomes, users can better grasp how to phrase their inputs.  
   首先，提供**示例**可以帮助用户了解如何有效地与人工智能交互。通过显示提示的具体实例及其结果，用户可以更好地掌握如何表达他们的输入。
2. Another useful approach is offering**templates** . Templates can serve as starting points, guiding users on how to structure their requests and enabling them to modify these templates according to their specific needs.  
   另一种有用的方法是提供**模板**。模板可以作为起点，指导用户如何构建他们的请求，并使他们能够根据自己的特定需求修改这些模板。
3. Additionally, **interactive tutorials** can significantly lower the barrier for new users. These tutorials can walk users through the process of crafting prompts and exploring AI features, making the learning curve less steep. For advanced users, such tutorials can introduce new functionalities and capabilities, keeping them engaged and informed.  
   此外，**交互式教程** 可以显着降低新用户的门槛。这些教程可以引导用户完成制作提示和探索人工智能功能的过程，从而使学习曲线不那么陡峭。对于高级用户来说，此类教程可以引入新的功能和能力，让他们保持参与和了解情况。

By incorporating these strategies, the prompt input experience can be made more intuitive and user-friendly, catering to both novices and experienced users alike.  
通过结合这些策略，可以使提示输入体验更加直观和用户友好，从而满足新手和经验丰富的用户的需求。

## Omnichannel input
全渠道输入

Modern AI models are omnichannel, capable of processing inputs in various formats beyond just text. To fully leverage these capabilities, apps should accommodate diverse user preferences for content submission.  
现代人工智能模型是全渠道的，能够处理除文本之外的各种格式的输入。为了充分利用这些功能，应用程序应该适应不同的用户提交内容的偏好。

By offering multiple input methods, such as **direct uploads** ,**recording tools** , l**ink integration** , and **drag-and-drop interfaces** , users can choose how they want to provide their content.  
通过提供多种输入方法，例如**直接上传** 、**记录工具** 、**链接集成** 和**拖放界面**，用户可以选择他们想要提供内容的方式。

This flexibility not only caters to individual preferences but also significantly enhances the overall user experience.  
这种灵活性不仅迎合了个人喜好，还显着增强了整体用户体验。 Whether a user wants to upload a file, record audio, link to external content, or simply drag and drop items, having these options available ensures that interacting with the AI is seamless and intuitive.  
无论用户想要上传文件、录制音频、链接到外部内容，还是只是拖放项目，提供这些选项都可以确保与 AI 的交互无缝且直观。

Providing such varied input methods allows users to engage with AI in the way that best suits their needs, ultimately making the technology more accessible and user-friendly.  
提供如此多样化的输入方法可以让用户以最适合自己需求的方式与人工智能互动，最终使该技术更易于使用和用户友好。

## Structured input with form
带有表单的结构化输入

Users often struggle to provide AI with comprehensive and organized information, which can lead to less accurate responses, especially in more complex tools.  
用户通常很难向人工智能提供全面且有组织的信息，这可能会导致响应不太准确，尤其是在更复杂的工具中。

Implementing structured input can alleviate this issue by **breaking down the required data into smaller, manageable chunks** .  
实施结构化输入可以通过将**所需数据分解为更小的、可管理的块来**缓解这个问题。

Structured input guides users through the process with clear steps, helping them understand what specific information is needed. This can be achieved through various elements such as  
结构化输入通过清晰的步骤引导用户完成整个过程，帮助他们了解需要哪些具体信息。这可以通过各种元素来实现，例如

- input fields, 输入字段，
- dropdown menus,  
  下拉菜单，
- checkboxes,  
  复选框,
- scales, and 尺度，以及
- other adjustable components.  
  其他可调节组件。

By offering these structured options, users are less likely to make errors, resulting in more relevant and predictable AI outcomes.  
通过提供这些结构化选项，用户犯错误的可能性较小，从而产生更相关和可预测的人工智能结果。

This approach not only simplifies the data entry process for users but also enhances the overall effectiveness of AI, ensuring that the responses generated are accurate and tailored to the user's needs.  
这种方法不仅简化了用户的数据输入过程，还增强了人工智能的整体有效性，确保生成的响应准确且适合用户的需求。

## Selecting existing content
选择现有内容

Integrating AI with existing products opens up new possibilities, particularly in leveraging context.  
将人工智能与现有产品集成开辟了新的可能性，特别是在利用环境方面。

In tools like Notion and VS Code, **users can select text and choose or type commands such as "summarize"** or "translate." This functionality eliminates the need to create inputs from scratch, significantly speeding up AI interactions.  
在 Notion 和 VS Code 等工具中，**用户可以选择文本并选择或键入"摘要"或"翻译"等命令**。此功能消除了从头开始创建输入的需要，从而显着加快了人工智能交互速度。

Another implementation of this idea is within structured content. For instance, Framer offers AI options through**a right-click menu within a frame** , while Arc provides **AI-generated summaries when hovering over a link** .  
这个想法的另一个实现是在结构化内容中。例如，Framer 通过**框架内的右键菜单** 提供 AI 选项，而Arc**在将鼠标悬停在链接上时提供AI 生成的摘要**。

These contextual integrations streamline the user experience by making AI tools more accessible and intuitive, ultimately enhancing productivity and interaction.  
这些上下文集成使人工智能工具更易于访问和直观，从而简化了用户体验，最终提高了生产力和交互性。

## Loading results 加载结果

Users can quickly become confused or frustrated if a loading indicator isn't visible, often assuming the app has frozen.  
如果加载指示器不可见，用户很快就会感到困惑或沮丧，通常假设应用程序已冻结。

Traditional looped animations without clear progress indication are generally inadequate for AI applications, especially for tasks taking longer than five seconds. To address this, AI tools have adopted various tactics to show progress and ease user concerns.  
没有明确进度指示的传统循环动画通常不适用于人工智能应用程序，特别是对于耗时超过五秒的任务。为了解决这个问题，人工智能工具采取了各种策略来显示进展并缓解用户的担忧。

## Progress indicator 进度指示器

In the world of AI, borrowing strategies from other successful apps has proven to be a smart move.  
在人工智能领域，借鉴其他成功应用程序的策略已被证明是明智之举。

One familiar tactic is the **progress indicator** , a simple yet powerful tool that shows users how much of their task is completed. This visual reassurance that things are moving forward --- even if slowly --- encourages patience and keeps frustration at bay.  
一种熟悉的策略是**进度指示器**，这是一种简单但功能强大的工具，可以向用户显示任务的完成情况。这种视觉上的保证表明事情正在向前发展------即使是缓慢的------鼓励耐心并防止沮丧。

Midjourney and ChatGPT have taken this a step further, creating a more dynamic user experience.  
Midjourney和ChatGPT更进一步，创造了更加动态的用户体验。 Instead of merely waiting for a task to finish, they let users peek behind the curtain.  
他们不是仅仅等待任务完成，而是让用户窥视幕后。

Midjourney, for instance, offers snapshots of its creative process, **allowing users to see a blurred preview of their results** as they evolve.   
例如， Midjourney提供了其创作过程的快照，**使用户能够在其发展过程中看到其结果的模糊预览**。**ChatGPT delivers answers word by word** , enabling users to start reading and engaging with the content almost immediately.  
**ChatGPT逐字提供答案**，使用户几乎可以立即开始阅读和参与内容。 This not only makes the waiting time feel shorter but also gives users the power to stop and tweak their prompts, making the entire process more interactive and efficient.  
这不仅使等待时间感觉更短，而且还使用户能够停止并调整提示，使整个过程更具互动性和效率。

## Updates on a current action
当前操作的更新

Some AI applications go even further, needing to fetch information from external sources or perform web searches. These steps can add significant time to the process, and it's not feasible to generate intermediate results.  
一些人工智能应用程序甚至更进一步，需要从外部来源获取信息或执行网络搜索。这些步骤可能会显着增加流程时间，并且生成中间结果是不可行的。

To manage user expectations and keep them informed, **providing progressive updates about these background activities** --- such as showing the number of pages searched --- can make the wait more tolerable and transparent.  
为了管理用户的期望并让他们了解情况，**提供有关这些后台活动的渐进式更新**（例如显示搜索的页面数）可以使等待变得更加可容忍和透明。

## Presenting AI results 展示人工智能结果

The way AI presents results varies widely, depending on the context and the type of application. We're witnessing an evolution where some presentation patterns are gaining traction, while others are fading into away.  
人工智能呈现结果的方式差异很大，具体取决于上下文和应用程序类型。我们正在见证一场演变，一些演示模式正在获得关注，而另一些则正在逐渐消失。

## Text results 文本结果

The simplest method for presenting large language model (LLM) results is through **text in a chat interface** .  
呈现大语言模型的最简单方法（LLM ）结果是通过**聊天界面中的文本**进行的。

This universal approach works well for most use cases, offering a great deal of flexibility. However, it can sometimes be inefficient. To enhance the appeal of text results, breaking them into smaller chunks and categorizing them makes the information more scannable and digestible.  
这种通用方法适用于大多数用例，提供了很大的灵活性。然而，它有时可能效率低下。为了增强文本结果的吸引力，将它们分成更小的块并对它们进行分类，使信息更易于浏览和理解。

In context-driven environments, like Notion, displaying results alongside the original text allows users to compare and decide whether to accept the changes, without altering the original content.  
在上下文驱动的环境中，例如Notion ，将结果与原始文本一起显示可以让用户比较并决定是否接受更改，而无需更改原始内容。

## Good AI interface design: Best practices
良好的人工智能界面设计：最佳实践

When you're working with AI, there are a few important things to keep in mind, as mentioned in the People + AI Guidebook.  
当您使用人工智能时，需要记住一些重要的事情，如《人与人工智能指南》中所述。

## 1 Determine if AI adds value
1 确定人工智能是否增加价值

AI is a powerful tool, but it excels in specific tasks. Before jumping into AI development, **assess whether your product or feature truly benefits from AI integration** .  
人工智能是一种强大的工具，但它在特定任务上表现出色。在开始人工智能开发之前，**请评估您的产品或功能是否真正受益于人工智能集成**。

AI shines in areas like  
人工智能在以下领域大放异彩

- content recommendations, 内容推荐，
- future predictions, 未来的预测，
- language comprehension, and  
  语言理解力，以及
- image recognition. 图像识别。

However, if your focus is on predictability, transparency, or human control, a simpler rule-based solution might be more effective.  
但是，如果您关注的是可预测性、透明度或人工控制，那么更简单的基于规则的解决方案可能会更有效。

## 2 Set the right expectations
2 设定正确的期望

Transparency is crucial for AI-powered products. Given that AI operates on probabilities, errors or unexpected results are always possible.  
透明度对于人工智能驱动的产品至关重要。鉴于人工智能根据概率进行操作，错误或意外结果总是有可能发生。

Building trust requires **honesty about both the strengths and weaknesses of your system** . While acknowledging potential mistakes might initially lower trust in a single prediction, it fosters long-term reliability as users understand the limitations and avoid over-reliance on the AI.  
建立信任需要**诚实地了解系统的优点和缺点**。虽然承认潜在的错误最初可能会降低对单个预测的信任度，但随着用户了解其局限性并避免过度依赖人工智能，它会促进长期可靠性。

## 3 Explain the benefit, not the technology
3 解释好处，而不是技术

When presenting your AI-enhanced product, emphasize the benefits it brings to the user experience rather than the technical details.  
在展示人工智能增强产品时，强调它给用户体验带来的好处，而不是技术细节。

Even if your AI is groundbreaking, **users are more interested in how it improves their experience** . Conduct [user studies](https://www.pragmaticcoders.com/services/product-design/ux-research-services) to determine the right level of technical explanation --- what do users actually need to know to use your product effectively?  
即使你的人工智能是开创性的，**用户也更感兴趣的是它如何改善他们的体验** 。进行[用户研究](https://www.pragmaticcoders.com/services/product-design/ux-research-services)以确定正确的技术解释水平------用户实际上需要知道什么才能有效地使用你的产品？

## 4 Be accountable for errors
4 对错误负责

AI isn't perfect, and planning for errors from the start is essential. This involves anticipating potential mistakes and their impacts, and being upfront with users about these possibilities.  
人工智能并不完美，从一开始就针对错误做好计划至关重要。这包括预测潜在的错误及其影响，并提前向用户告知这些可能性。

Consider the stakes for your users and the consequences of both false positives and false negatives. To **manage errors** , provide explanations, offer manual controls when AI fails, and ensure robust customer support.  
考虑用户的风险以及误报和漏报的后果。**管理错误** 、提供解释、在人工智能失败时提供手动控制，并确保强大的客户支持。

## 5 Invest early in good practices
5 尽早投资于良好实践

**Successful AI products are built on high-quality data** . Poor data planning and collection can lead to "data cascades" --- issues that only become apparent later in development, negatively affecting user experience.  
**成功的人工智能产品建立在高质量的数据之上** 。糟糕的数据规划和收集可能会导致"数据级联"------这些问题在开发后期才会变得明显，从而对用户体验产生负面影响。

To avoid these pitfalls, collect data in batches, accept some "noisy" data (data with imperfections), plan for ongoing data maintenance, and collaborate with domain experts to ensure your data accurately reflects the real world.  
为了避免这些陷阱，请批量收集数据，接受一些"噪音"数据（有缺陷的数据），计划持续的数据维护，并与领域专家合作，以确保您的数据准确反映现实世界。
