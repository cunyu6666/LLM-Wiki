Empathic Voice Interface (EVI)

##### Introducing EVI 2

Our flagship model, EVI 2, introduces a new voice-to-voice AI model architecture. EVI 2 can converse rapidly and fluently, understand a user's tone of voice, and generate any tone of voice. It's capable of emulating a wide range of personalities, accents, and speaking styles. It can replace or integrate with LLMs.

> 来源: [Learn More](http://hume.ai/blog/introducing-evi2)

##### Design synthetic voices and personalities

Emulate the ideal personality for your use case with flexible prompting and voice modulation tools. We developed a novel voice modulation approach that allows anyone to adjust EVI 2's 10+ base voices along a number of continuous scales, including femininity, nasality, pitch, and more.

Optimized for Human Well-Being

##### Build AI voices people can trust

EVI 2 excels at anticipating and adapting to users' preferences, made possible by its special training for emotional intelligence. Its pleasant and fun personality is a result of this deeper alignment with human values.

In deploying this technology, we require developers to adhere to the guidelines of The Hume Initiative, a non-profit that sets the first concrete guidelines for empathic AI.
