## 🏆 产品推荐

### 免费的 Figma 矢量变形插件：Warp It

该插件可以在 Figma 中将文字、矢量形状，进行二次变形调节。

[https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/923820065156924054/warp-it](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/923820065156924054/warp-it)

### 图像素材插件：High-quality photos

可以通过插件轻松搜索并插入高质量图片到自己的项目中

[https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/893426972424060133/photos](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/893426972424060133/photos)

![](https://moonvy.com/blog/_assets/5d44a04a2877e5f9.png)

### AI 生成插件：Fast Canvas AI

在 Figma 中可以使用 Fast Canvas AI 插件生成图标、背景等图案素材

[https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1334452585172921151/fast-canvas-ai](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1334452585172921151/fast-canvas-ai)

![](https://moonvy.com/blog/_assets/d21a52c5e547502.png)

### 导入多图像插件：Multi images importer

根据规则将多图像从本地存储导入到 Figma 设计文件中快速替换

[https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1254358488915327979/multi-images-importer](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1254358488915327979/multi-images-importer)

![](https://moonvy.com/blog/_assets/c9660f7824d6cd19.png)

### 颜色生成器插件：Foundation: Color Generator

使用 Foundation: Color Generator 工具包，可以基于 Material、Atlassian、Orbit 和 Ant Design 等优秀设计系统快速创建色彩调色板，同时可以将色彩方案添加到样式指南中。

[https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1024452006068794933/foundation-color-generator](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1024452006068794933/foundation-color-generator)

![](https://moonvy.com/blog/_assets/a10f2f5369fad967.png)
