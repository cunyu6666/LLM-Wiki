Website for real estate agency in Dubai. Demo project.


This project is an attempt to rethink the approach to creating a website for real estate agencies, where, as a rule, the agency itself is more important than the objects it offers. In my work I wanted to shift this emphasis, to rethink the values of the company. In my opinion, the object, product or service is more important than the person who produces or provides it. Despite our age of brandomania, it seems to me that consumer values of buyers will be reversed very soon.

My Role: UX/UI Designer, Web Designer, Research

Goal: To shift the focus from the agency to the properties it represents.

<br />

Objective: To make the search, selection and study of objects (projects) more convenient and easier, bringing the client and the property closer by visually (but not substantively) reducing the agency's intermediation between the seller and the buyer.

Timeline: August 2024

The name Alhiat comes from the Arabic language and translates to life. This name accurately reflects what Alhiat real estate agency primarily wants to give to its clients. The task is not just to sell a person an object, but to give him the opportunity to live with pleasure.
