## 摘要

Lovable 创始人 Felix Haas 总结的 AI 时代高绩效团队 7 条原则：**像创始人思考 / 雇态度 / 保持好奇 / 让资深者变深 / 杀死 ego / 超出预期地在乎 / 先发后改**。核心论点——AI 极大放大个体杠杆，组织形态必须从"管理员工"转向"招募 owner"。

## 7 条原则速查

| # | 原则 | 一句话 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Think like a founder** | 不能指派 ownership，只能雇天然会抢的人 |
| 2 | **Hire for attitude, not skills** | AI 时代好奇心 / 韧性 > 技能 |
| 3 | **Stay genuinely curious** | 用 AI ≠ 痴迷 AI；好奇心复利最快 |
| 4 | **Let senior people go deep again** | 资深 operator + AI = 现代公司最强组合 |
| 5 | **Kill ego before it kills momentum** | 最快团队不争功劳，只关心什么有效 |
| 6 | **Care more than expected** | 顶尖共性：看到别人忽略的东西并修掉 |
| 7 | **Ship, then improve** | Shipping is simply how they learn |

## 核心金句

> You can't assign ownership. You can only hire people who naturally take it.

> Curiosity compounds faster than almost anything else.

> A highly experienced operator with AI is one of the most powerful combinations in modern companies.

> Shipping is simply how they learn.
